Toucan

Toucans are a part of the Ramphastidae family. They are omnivores and can weigh up to 680 grams depending on the species.

Lovebird

Lovebirds are a part of the Psittaciform family. They are omnivores and can weigh up to 60 grams.

Parrot

Parrots are a part of the Psittaciform family. They are omnivores and can weigh up to 64 grams to 1.6 kilograms.

Budgerigar

Budgerigars can also known as parakeets (depending on the species), are a part of the Psittaciform family. They are omnivores and can weigh up to 30 grams to 40 grams.
